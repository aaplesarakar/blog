Streamline Paperwork with eSign Services
eSign enables simple, efficient, and secure sharing of digitally signed electronic documents
By CSC VLE

The eSign service is designed to eliminate your paperwork challenges. As a game-changing tool in today’s corporate landscape and e-governance, eSign not only benefits the environment by reducing paper use but also simplifies processes for citizens. Let’s dive into what this transformative service entails.

What is eSign?

eSign is an innovative solution that facilitates easy, efficient, and secure sharing of digitally signed electronic documents through e-KYC authentication. Compliant with India's IT Act, it allows Aadhaar holders to legally sign forms and documents electronically, anytime and anywhere, without the need for manual signatures.

This service allows users to sign electronic documents digitally without requiring a physical digital signature device. The traditional process of obtaining a Digital Signature Certificate via printed applications with ink signatures and supporting documents is no longer necessary.

Benefits

• Available for use anytime, anywhere
• Fast processing with straight-through workflows
• Security and authentication powered by Aadhaar e-KYC
• Cross-device compatibility
• Enhances customer satisfaction
• Reduces costs by minimizing paper use
• Improves operational efficiency
• Convenient and user-friendly
• Affordable pricing
• Real-time support and doorstep service provided by CSC

Applications of eSign:

• Digital Locker: Self-attestation
• Taxation: ID applications and e-filing
• Finance: Account opening in banks and post offices
• Transport: Applications for driving license renewal and vehicle registration
• Certificates: Applications for birth, caste, marriage, income certificates, etc.
• Passport: Issuance and reissue applications
• Telecom: New connection applications
• Education: Enrollment and exam applications
• Parliament: Submission of parliamentary questions

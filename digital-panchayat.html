Digital Panchayat to Empower Jharkhand through CSCs
Government services made more accessible via CSCs in Panchayats under the Digital Panchayat Scheme
By CSC VLE

The Jharkhand Government, in partnership with the Panchayati Raj Department, is dedicated to speeding up development and public welfare programs in the rural regions of the state. To support this mission, the government has introduced the Digital Panchayat initiative, which focuses on digitizing Panchayats via Common Service Centres (CSCs). The initiative aims to provide crucial government services to every village, fostering comprehensive development across the state.

Under the Digital Panchayat Scheme, the Panchayati Raj Department is making government services easily accessible by establishing CSCs within Panchayats. Through these centers, villagers can obtain important documents and services directly from their Panchayats, cutting out middlemen and increasing transparency. For just ₹30, citizens can now apply for services such as residence certificates, income certificates, caste certificates, and pensions—a significant and historic move towards simplifying access to public services.

Additionally, the Jharkhand government is expanding services by offering postal services, including speed post and registered post facilities, directly in Panchayats. This means villagers can now avail themselves of postal services right from their village, further enhancing convenience. By August 2024, approximately 9.7 lakh digital transactions had been completed through these CSCs, marking a major milestone in Jharkhand's digital transformation journey.

This innovative Digital Panchayat Scheme is revolutionizing development in the state's remote villages. Gone are the days when villagers had to travel to block offices for government services. Now, services such as banking, residential certificates, income certificates, and birth certificates are available at the village level. This initiative has not only curbed corruption but has also dramatically improved the speed and transparency of service delivery.

Jharkhand’s digital future is bright, and the Digital Panchayat Scheme is a pivotal step in empowering the rural population through technology.
